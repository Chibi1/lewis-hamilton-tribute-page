Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton

The young Brit who surprised us all

A timeline of Lewis Hamilton's Life

  • 1985 - Born in Stevenage, Hertsfordshire
  • 1991 - Father bought him his first go-kart
  • 1993 - First go-kart race
  • 1998 - Hamilton wins the Super One series and his second British championship
  • 2001 - Joins the British Formula Renault Winter Series and finishes 5th
  • 2006 - Due to his success in Formula Three, he joined GP2 team ART Grand Prix and wins the championship at his first attempt
  • 2007 - The following year he joins Maclaren
  • 2008 - Becomes the youngest ever Formula 1 world champion at age 23
  • 2013 - Joins with the Mercedes-Benz works team and gets his first victory with them at the Hungarian Grand Prix
  • 2014 - Second World Title
  • 2015 - Third World Title
  • 2016 - Comes runner up to his teammate, Nico Rosberg
  • 2017 - Fourth World Title
  • 2018 - Currently leading the championship for a fifth World Title
